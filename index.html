Taj Mahal

Agra,uttar pradesh

The Taj Mahal is an iconic ivory-white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Its breathtaking beauty, intricate architecture, and symbolic significance make it one of the world's most celebrated wonders.
